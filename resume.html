About me

Hello everyone! I'm Lory Ann Joy Española, a vibrant 21-year-old with a heart full of passion and a soul that yearns for adventure. Born on October 2, 2003, in the charming town of San Dionisio, Iloilo, I've always been drawn to the simple joys of life. Growing up in Brgy. Sua, I learned the value of community and the beauty of nature.

My educational journey began at Pinay Espinosa Elementary School, where I discovered a love for learning and a thirst for knowledge. I continued my studies at Hda. Conchita National High School, where I blossomed into a confident young woman, eager to explore the world. Now, I'm pursuing my dreams at Northern Iloilo State University, embracing the challenges and opportunities that come with higher education.

But my life isn't just about academics. I'm a firm believer in the power of movement and expression. Dancing is my escape, a way to let loose and celebrate life. Cycling through the countryside allows me to connect with nature and appreciate the beauty that surrounds me. And on the volleyball court, I find a sense of teamwork and camaraderie that brings me immense joy.

Beyond sports, I have a passion for nurturing and caring. Whether it's tending to my beloved pets or simply lending a helping hand to those in need, I find fulfillment in making a difference. In the kitchen, I love experimenting with flavors and creating culinary masterpieces that bring people together. And there's nothing more rewarding than forging new friendships and sharing laughter with those I love.

Traveling and road trips are my ultimate escapes. There's something magical about exploring new places, immersing myself in different cultures, and creating memories that will last a lifetime. And let's be honest, who doesn't love a good night's sleep? It's my way of recharging and preparing for the adventures that lie ahead.

My culinary cravings are diverse and delicious. Avocados and mangoes are my go-to fruits, while ice cream, pizza, buko/egg pie, crinkles, chocolate cake, chocolates, and milk tea are my comfort food staples. My parents, Andy Java Española and Lorly Aligato Española, have instilled in me a love for family and a strong work ethic. They are my constant source of support and inspiration.

I'm excited about the future and all the possibilities it holds. I'm eager to learn, grow, and make a positive impact on the world. With my infectious energy, unwavering determination, and a heart full of love, I know I can achieve anything I set my mind to. This is Lory Ann Joy Española, and this is my story.
